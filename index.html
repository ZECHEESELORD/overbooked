The Details

How will you read with us?

We have two ways you can enjoy books with us!

Read on your own time:

Report your thoughts about the book on the google form after each week. The book will be brought up in discussions during the following Zoom meeting.

Read with us:

You can also read with us! We will be providing PDFs of the books, as well as the names and titles of the books if you wish to borrow them. The information will be posted to our official communication channels.
